Governance

Admin-only pages for visibility into usage and health, to support capacity planning and guardrails.

Open governance

What to expect

  • Usage snapshots (p95 CPU/memory/disk) and active users over time.
  • Top users by active deployments and running tasks.
  • Retention is limited (default 14 days) to keep storage bounded.

When you need more resources

  • Use the usage charts + snapshots to show sustained CPU/mem/disk pressure.
  • Call out growth in “active users (24h)” and “active deployments”.
  • Use “Top users” for collaboration (not blame): large labs can be moved/scheduled.
